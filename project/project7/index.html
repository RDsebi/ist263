Welcome to Half-Marathon for College students

This website dedicated to helping college students learn how to manage their time while training for a half marathon. Running a half marathon is a challenging goal, but it's also a rewarding experience that can help you build confidence, improve your physical fitness, and create lasting memories. And as a college student, you have the added challenge of balancing your running schedule with academic responsibilities and a busy social life. That's why this site was created. We provide training plans, nutrition advice, and tips on how to make the most of your time and achieve your goal of running 13.1 miles while still thriving in college. So, whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting out, join us on this journey and let's make it happen!

Who am I

My name is Sebastian Rodriguez and I recently completed my first half marathon on April 16th. As a student at Syracuse University, I faced a unique challenge in trying to balance my training for the half marathon while juggling my academic responsibilities, extracurricular activities, and social life. During my research on how to train for a half marathon, I found plenty of useful information on nutrition, training techniques, and more, but I struggled to find advice on time management.

With a training schedule that could take up to two hours a day, I knew that I needed to find a way to balance my time effectively. As a result, I decided to create a website that would provide others with tips and strategies that I personally used to manage my time effectively while training for a half marathon. My goal is to help other college students find a healthy balance between their academics, social life, and fitness goals.
